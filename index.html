FAQs

Questions about Phármakon?

How does Phármakon work? +

You upload medical documents or type your health history directly. Our AI extracts structured information — diagnoses, medications, lab results, providers — and builds a living clinical profile around you. Every new input improves the profile and sharpens the AI's answers.

Is my health data safe? +

Yes. Phármakon runs on HIPAA-eligible infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, full audit logging, and signed BAAs with every vendor that touches your data. Your data is never sold or shared with insurers, and never used to train AI models without your explicit opt-in.

What documents can I upload? +

Phármakon accepts PDFs, JPGs, PNGs, and scanned images — including lab results, discharge summaries, EOBs, referral letters, imaging reports, prescriptions, and vaccination records. If your provider uses MyChart or another FHIR-compatible portal, you can import records directly.

Is Phármakon a replacement for my doctor? +

No — and by design. Phármakon informs, surfaces patterns, and helps you prepare for care. It does not diagnose or recommend treatments. Every insight is a starting point for a conversation with your provider. When something warrants urgent attention, Phármakon tells you to seek care immediately.
